Gender Equality in Leonila Hill

Leonila Hill is a barangay for all genders. Everyone is welcome to the community. In the barangay, residents know their rights a part of the community. Respect is apparent among men, women, and LGBT people in Leonila Hill. Different types of people correlate with each other. The barangay advocates gender eqaulity in their community.

A provision about gender equality need not to be pushed in the barangay of Leonilla Hill because of the inate respect of the residents among each other. Men and women treat each other as equals in the community without biases nor preconcieved notions.

To promote gender equality, all that is needed is the nature of humanity which is to respect each other. If there is respect between genders, equality will follow. The mission of the barangay is to advocate respect towards gender equality.

Leonila Hill Barangay, Baguio City
