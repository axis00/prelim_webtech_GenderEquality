History & Geography

History

Leonila Hill Barangay was originally called Sunrise Subdivision that was later acquired by the Government Insurance Service System (GSIS) during the term of President Carlos P. Garcia. Subsequently, the subdivision was renamed Leonila Hill Subdivision after the name of First Lady Leonila Garcia.

After the City was divided into different barangay, the upper part of Leonila hill Subdivision became West Bayan Park Barangay since it is located West of Bayan Park Village. Lower portion of the subdivision became North SAnitary Camp Barangay.

However, in response to a petition from the Barangay Council, the City passed an Ordinance Numbered 62, Series of 1987 renaming West Bayan Park Barangay back to Leonila Hill Barangay.

Officially, Leonila Hill Subdivision was a vacation place by the original homeowners from Manila. The proximity of the subdivision to the City Proper made it deal for vacation homes, considering that, it is not far from the Central Business area. The surrounding hills that are stll lush and verdant that provides clean environment and nice view.

As the years went by, homeowners sold their houses to individuals who decided to settle for good.

Geography

Leonila Hill Barangay is one of the thirteen (13) barangays of Aurora Hill. It belongs to District 9-5.

It occupies a total land area of 12.6 hectares more or less. It is bouded on the North of Sanitary Camp Baranga; on the South of Bayan Park Village Barangay; on the West of San Antonio Village Barangay and on the East of Ambiong-Baguio Barangay.

It is approximately two (2) kilometers away from the City Central Business District (CBD).

VISION

A Barangay that is independent, effective, and efficient, and worthy of public trust and confidence. Thereby providing quality, ethical, accesible and cost-efficient service to our people and is willing and able to answer the call of public service.

MISSION

Provide an honest, efficient, effective, expeditive and accessible service under wholesome environment for all people of the barangay.
